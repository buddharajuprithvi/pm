Alzi Tech

Bridging Legacy Industries with the Digital World

The idea of starting Alzi Tech was to address the digital gap in traditional sectors like construction and healthcare. Facing low technical literacy and resistance to change, we built a modular, configurable platform designed for ease of use. Leading a team of six, we delivered scalable solutions to clients such as SriKanya Steels and Susrutha Hospitals, improving operational efficiency and accelerating their digital adoption.

Srikanya Steels

https://www.srikanyasteels.com

E-Commerce + ERP Platform for Construction Materials


I spearheaded the team to build a cloud based platform for Srikanya Steels to tackle key challenges in sales, inventory management, and customer transparency within the construction materials supply chain.

Defining the product vision and crafted the roadmap I have partnered with cross-functional teams to plan, build, and deliver a platform (www.srikanyasteels.com) that streamlined the entire customer journey—from product discovery and quotation generation to digital approvals, dispatch tracking, and payment settlement. Key features included real-time inventory management, rule-based approval workflows, and flexible payment configurations to improve usability and reduce operational bottlenecks.

On the business side, the system catered to diverse roles such as Sales, Manufacturing, Logistics, Finance, HR, and Leadership. The system is also powered with a customizable analytical dashboard where the users could easily select various viewing parameters that provided visibility into data such as order cycles, credit utilization, sales performance, profitability, and various other. These insights empowered the business team to make faster, data-informed decisions and significantly improved efficiency and transparency.

Impact :
  • 40% reduction in quotation turnaround time
  • 25% increase in repeat customer orders within 3 months
  • 60% reduction in overall order lifecycle duration
This platform established a scalable and transparent foundation for digital procurement in the construction industry.
