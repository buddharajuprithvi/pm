Alzi Tech

Bridging Legacy Industries with the Digital World

The idea of starting Alzi Tech was to address the digital gap in traditional sectors like construction and healthcare. Facing low technical literacy and resistance to change, we built a modular, configurable platform designed for ease of use. Leading a team of six, we delivered scalable solutions to clients such as SriKanya Steels and Susrutha Hospitals, improving operational efficiency and accelerating their digital adoption.

Srikanya Steels

www.srikanyasteels.com

E-Commerce + ERP Platform for Construction Materials

I led the development of a cloud-based platform for Srikanya Steels to address critical challenges in sales operations, inventory management, and customer transparency—streamlining the construction materials supply chain and improving end-to-end efficiency.

By conducting 40+ stakeholder interviews, analyzing existing workflows, and service blueprinting to identify key friction points, I have built a comprehensive product roadmap to design and develop www.srikanyasteels.com - a platform that streamlines the end-to-end customer journey from product discovery and quotation generation to digital approvals, dispatch tracking, and payment settlement.

On the business side, the system catered to diverse roles such as Sales, Manufacturing, Logistics, Finance, HR, and Leadership. The system is also powered with a customizable analytical dashboard where the users could easily select various viewing parameters that provided visibility into data such as order Turn-Around-Time (TAT), credit utilization, sales performance, profitability, and various other. These insights empowered the business team to make faster, data-informed decisions and significantly improved efficiency and transparency.

Impact :
  • 40% reduction in quotation turnaround time
  • 25% increase in repeat customer orders within 3 months
  • 60% reduction in overall order lifecycle duration
This platform established a scalable and transparent foundation for digital procurement in the construction industry.

Susrutha Hospitals

Hospital Resource Management & OR Booking for 3rd parties

To streamline hospital operations and enhance patient care at Susrutha Hospitals, I led the development of a centralized platform for resource management, operation scheduling, and OR (Operation Room) booking—particularly tailored for third-party doctors.

Entering into healthcare industry "an ecosystem new to me", I conducted extensive on-the-ground discovery by shadowing and interviewing doctors, nurses, and administrative staff to map existing workflows and uncover inefficiencies. Leveraging these insights, I have come up with a Product Requirements Document (PRD) and a project plan that prioritized usability, automation, and alignment with real-world hospital needs.

The resulting platform consolidates key scheduling components—such as OR allocation, blood work coordination, equipment provisioning, and staff availability—into a unified interface. By reducing manual overhead, optimizing resource utilization, and improving coordination, the solution minimizes administrative burden and supports higher-quality, more efficient patient care.

Impact :
  • 65% reduction in manual efforts in scheduling operations
  • 30% improvement in resource utilization
  • Enhanced patient care through streamlined processes
