Product Teardown

Following my passion for product management, I have worked on several case studies to hone my skills and apply my knowledge in real-world scenarios. Here are some of the case studies I have worked on:

www.splitwise.com

Link
I have conducted an in-depth product teardown of Splitwise, mapping the end-to-end user journey, identifying friction points, and analyzing strategic levers for improving engagement and retention. Through my research and competitive benchmarking, I have come up with a few potential high-impact initiatives (along with sample Figma screens and user journeys) to enhance the user experience, including:
  1. Launching a mobile-native itemized expenses feature
  2. Integrating in-app payments to retain users within the platform
  3. Enabling bill scanning with image processing for automated transaction entry
  4. Linking bank transaction data to streamline expense recall.

www.myfitnesspal.com

Link
A product teardown of MyFitnessPal, evaluating market dynamics, competitive landscape, and target user segments to identify strategic opportunities for increasing engagement and retention. Leveraging user insights and gap analysis, I have come up with three high-impact initiatives:
  1. Integrating partner restaurant menu data for accurate calorie tracking
  2. Implementing culturally localized and searchable recipe recommendations
  3. Enabling in-gym equipment guidance through NFC/QR code integration.
